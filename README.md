---

## 🎯 The Problem

Every founder has been there: you have an idea, you're excited, you start building —  
and 3 months later you realize nobody wants it.

**Ideator exists to kill bad ideas fast and double down on good ones.**

Instead of guessing, founders use Ideator to:
- Score their idea against market realities
- Research competitors in minutes, not days
- Validate demand before building an MVP
